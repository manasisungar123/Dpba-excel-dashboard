The Hospital Emergency Room dashboard shows overall ER performance and patient flow during the selected period.
The average waiting time indicates that patients spend a noticeable amount of time before receiving care, and delays are a major concern compared to on-time cases.
Even with these delays, patient satisfaction remains generally positive, showing that the quality of care is good once treatment begins. 
The admission status reflects effective triage, with a balanced split between admitted and non-admitted patients. 
Gender-wise analysis shows a nearly equal distribution of male and female patients.
Age-wise trends indicate that younger and older age groups visit the ER more frequently, suggesting higher care needs in these groups.
Department referrals are mainly concentrated in general care and orthopedic services, highlighting areas with higher demand.
Overall, the dashboard suggests stable ER operations, with the key improvement area being reduction in delays and waiting time to improve efficiency and patient experience.
